About Gyan Jyoti VidhyaKunj Ma. Vi.

Established in 2053 B.S., Gyan Jyoti VidhyaKunj Ma. Vi. is a renowned educational institution located at Pokhara Metropolitan City–32, Tallo Gagan Gaunda, Kaski. Under the visionary leadership of Principal Mr. Arjun Prasad Adhikari, Vice Principal Mr. Mitrabandhu Subedi, and Program Coordinator Mr. Tankaraj Subedi, the school has continued to provide quality education and holistic development. With nearly 350 students, the school offers education from Montessori to Grade 10. The school is equipped with a Science Lab, Computer Lab, Library, Canteen, Garden Hall, and a spacious playground. Students actively participate in singing, dancing, sports, art competitions, music classes, computer classes, abacus classes, and many more activities. Gyan Jyoti VidhyaKunj Ma. Vi. proudly maintains a 100% SEE and BLE pass record and continues to shape responsible and confident citizens.

"Education for Excellence and Character Building."

World Environment Day

>

As part of the World Environment Day celebration, students enthusiastically participated in an environmental awareness campaign by carrying creative slogans and placards highlighting the importance of protecting our planet. The rally aimed to inspire people to conserve natural resources, maintain cleanliness, and work together to build a sustainable future.

On the occasion of World Environment Day, the students and teachers of GyanJyoti VidhyaKunj Ma. Vi. organized an environmental awareness rally to promote cleanliness, tree plantation, and environmental conservation. Carrying informative placards and slogans, the participants encouraged the community to protect nature and work together for a cleaner, greener, and healthier future. The program helped develop environmental responsibility and social awareness among students.

>

On the occasion of World Environment Day, our respected Principal, Mr. Arjun Prasad Adhikari, actively participated in the tree plantation program along with students and teachers. His involvement inspired everyone to protect the environment and work together towards creating a greener, cleaner, and more sustainable future. 🌱🌍
