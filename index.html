About Gyan Jyoti VidhyaKunj Ma.

Established in 2053 B.S., Gyan Jyoti VidhyaKunj Ma. Vi. is a renowned educational institution located at Pokhara Metropolitan City–32, Tallo Gagan Gaunda, Kaski. Since its establishment, the school has been dedicated to providing quality education and nurturing young minds in a safe, disciplined, and inspiring environment. Under the visionary leadership of Principal Mr. Arjun Prasad Adhikari, along with Vice Principal Mr. Mitrabandhu Subedi and Program Coordinator Mr. Tankaraj Subedi, the school continues to strive for academic excellence and holistic development. With nearly 350 students, Gyan Jyoti VidhyaKunj Ma. Vi. offers education from Montessori, Nursery, LKG, and UKG to Grade 10. The school believes in fostering intellectual growth, creativity, and moral values to prepare students for future challenges. The school is equipped with modern facilities including a well-equipped Science Laboratory, Computer Laboratory, Library, Canteen, Garden Hall, and a large playground that supports both academic and extracurricular activities. Beyond academics, the school encourages students to participate in various activities and competitions such as singing, dancing, cultural programs, sports events, and art competitions. Special classes including Music Class, Computer Class, Art Class, Abacus Class, and engaging activities like children's games and rhymes are conducted to ensure the overall development of every child. Gyan Jyoti VidhyaKunj Ma. Vi. takes pride in its outstanding academic record, having achieved a 100% pass rate in SEE and BLE examinations in previous years. The school remains committed to shaping responsible, knowledgeable, and confident citizens who will contribute positively to society. "Education for Excellence and Character Building."
